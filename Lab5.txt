Briefly explain what dependency injection is and why it is beneficial?
-Dependency injection is the idea that one object's dependency is supplied by another. The injection is making it possible for the dependent object to use it.
It allows the facilitation of unit testing without having to mess with your code.


Briefly explain what unit tests are and why they are beneficial?
Unit testing is the idea of testing smaller pieces of code for their memory usage, their functionality and the testing for bugs within the code.
They important because they help developers and testers to work together without interference. As the developer continues their work, the tester test what they have so far
and both exchange feedback. This makes development process faster and smoother.
